About CGPA Calculator

Why I made this application?

After publishing result in each semester I had to calculate my CGPA and doing it each time using other website or in hand is a bit annoying. Moreover, most of the website doesn't fit with my university CGPA system.

So later on, I decided to make one. Previously I made an WPF - C# application (Windows Platform) for my personal use. But I realized that most of the people faces same problem that I faced previously. This is why I made this application to help who needs speacially for the students of East West University (EWU).
