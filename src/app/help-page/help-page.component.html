Ways to calculate GPA or CGPA
Step 1

1. Select Your University First

2. Go to Step 2

Step 2

1. Enter Current CGPA and current earned credit only if you want to calculate CGPA. For Calculating GPA it's optional.

2. Go to step 3

Step 3

1. Select Subjects Grade to enable corresponding credit field

2. Go to Step 4

Step 4

1. Click on the Button "Calculate GPA/CGPA"

2. Done: See Result Below the Button
